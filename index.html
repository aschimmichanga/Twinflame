About US
In these uncertain times, reaching out to the people around us is harder than ever. We're a group of freshmen Northeastern students who want to make getting to know our digital neighbors a little bit easier.

To that end, we've created a outdoors-based people-matching platform, Twin Flame. Users answer a series of questions in camping scenarios; we then use a custom-made algorithm to match them with whoever answered most similarly. We're hoping our survey will spark conversations and help some people find their 'twin flame'.

Get Matched!
‍

Our quiz takes about five minutes to complete. At the end, enter your full name and email address, and you'll see your match's name and email. Don't wait, get in touch and strike up a conversation!
